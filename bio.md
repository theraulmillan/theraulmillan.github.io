## BIO

Cybersecurity expert with 20 years of experience. My specialties include: development of strategies, plans, policies, processes, and IT security procedures; compliance with standards (NIST, SOX, ISO 27001); selection, implementation, management, and monitoring of technical and administrative security controls; [threat management](https://the.raulmillan.com/2018-05-15-amenazas/); security architecture; [critical infrastructure protection](https://the.raulmillan.com/2018-04-19-CI/); cloud security (design and execution); leading working groups in information security and incident response; and managing governance functions related to cybersecurity.

Responsible for strategic projects such as development of the [National Cybersecurity Strategy and Critical Infrastructure Protection](https://www.gacetaoficial.gob.pa/pdfTemp/27289_A/GacetaNo_27289a_20130517.pdf), [Panama's accession to the Budapest Convention on Cybercrime (Council of Europe)](http://gacetas.procuraduria-admon.gob.pa/27403-A_2013.pdf), securing the National Multi-Service Network of the State, and the establishment of the [Panama National Incident Response Center (CSIRT)](https://cert.pa/) among others.

Some of my most relevant assignments include management and oversight of Panama's National Incident Response Center ([CSIRT Panama](https://www.cert.pa/)), IT Security Unit Supervisor at the Panama Canal Authority, Systems Security Unit Manager at [Cable & Wireless Panama](https://www.cwpanama.com/), and Security Solutions Architect for the [Millicom-TIGO](https://www.millicom.com/) group.

I am a graduate of [Central American Technological University](http://www.unitec.edu/) (Honduras) with a degree in Information Systems Administration.

I hold the following professional certifications: [CISSP](https://www.isc2.org/Certifications/CISSP) (Certified Information Systems Security Professional), [CISM](http://www.isaca.org/Certification/CISM-Certified-Information-Security-Manager/Pages/default.aspx) (Certified Information Security Manager), [CISA](http://www.isaca.org/Certification/CISA-Certified-Information-Systems-Auditor/Pages/default.aspx) (Certified Information Systems Auditor), and [CEH](https://www.eccouncil.org/programs/certified-ethical-hacker-ceh/) (Certified Ethical Hacker)
